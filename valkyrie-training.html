Valkyrie Training

Workouts crafted for women that are SO MUCH MORE than just a workout!


Stemming from Norse mythology, the valkyrie are angelic warriors, depicted as gorgeous, strong women with magnificent angel wings, adorned in battle armor and weilding swords. To me, this is the perfect archetype for the modern woman who is inspiring to embody her best self.

It is an archetype of perfect balance between the feminine and masculine, or yin and yang, energies. (These are aspects of consciousness that exist within each person, they are not based on one's gender.) On one side is the grace-filled love and compassion of an angel and the soft beauty and sensuality of a woman. On the other there is the strength, willingness, courage and protective spirit of the warrior. The valkyrie exemplifies the magic of both extreme ends of the spectrum manifest within one person.



The greatest health and wholeness comes with inner balance, not when one aspect of our being is overindulged and the other atrophied. This training was thus designed to condition, empower and balance the inner masculine and feminine energies within each of us. We get to learn about these aspects of our consciounessness through the movement and musculature of our own bodies, learning the qualities of each, how they work together, and how to move between them with grace and skill. Each session involves breathwork, stretching, tai chi, kung fu-based martial arts practice including basic striking and kicking, dynamic tension, and freeform meditative movement. The format was also constructed with the intention of being an ideal combination of practices for the body to maintain optimal condition. This includes:

  • Full Body Stretching & Opening
  • Strength Training & Muscle Toning
  • Balance, Conditioning & Coordination
  • Grounded Poise & Posture
  • Meditative Movement
  • Mindful Breathing
  • Exploring & Harmonizing Inner Yin/Yang Energy
  • Cultivating Intimate Relationship with the Body

Empower yourself as a modern day Woman Warrior! Join a community of women supporting each other in our commitment to embody our best selves. As we do, we can bring more of us to how we show up in the world, empowered in our loving actions, becoming catalysts for positive change.
